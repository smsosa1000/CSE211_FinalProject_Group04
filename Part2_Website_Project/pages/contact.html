Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for an event?

You can register for events by visiting the Events Catalog page, selecting your desired event, and clicking the "Register Now" button. Follow the registration form to complete your sign-up.

Can I get a refund for event registration?

Refund policies vary by event. Please check the specific event details for refund information. Generally, refunds are available up to 7 days before the event, minus any processing fees.

How do I access my event dashboard?

You can access your dashboard by clicking the "Dashboard" link in the main navigation. Log in with your registered email and password to view your registered events, manage bookings, and track your event history.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for Egyptian accounts. Some events may also accept cash payments at the venue.

How can I become an event organizer?

If you're interested in organizing events through our platform, please contact our partnership team at partnerships@eventsx-gu.edu with details about your organization and event ideas.
